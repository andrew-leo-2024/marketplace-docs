## D. AINEFF DEPLOYMENT ARCHITECTURE

### ORF/ETLB/MCO Structural Constraints
- **ORF**: Every AI-generated recommendation, board briefing, and automated decision carries an Obligation Record. When the system recommends a supply chain rerouting, the ORF binds the decision to a specific outcome commitment with measurable success criteria.
- **ETLB**: At the moment any AI system executes an action with financial, legal, or operational consequence (trade execution, contract modification, employee termination recommendation), a named human is cryptographically bound as the liability bearer. No "the AI did it" defense.
- **MCO**: Every AI system deployed in the enterprise carries a mortality date. Maximum lifespan: 36 months without re-authorization. Every automated workflow, every model deployment, every integration -- all expire. No zombie systems accumulating undocumented authority.

### E-AEGL Governance Hardening
- **Sub-10ms Policy Enforcement**: Every AI inference call routed through the Enhancement Layer is validated against enterprise policy in under 10ms. PII detection, compliance checks, cost limits, and authority verification happen before output reaches any downstream system.
- **SHA-256 Hash-Chained Audit Logs**: Every AI decision, every board briefing data point, every billing reconciliation action is immutably logged. Regulators in any jurisdiction can receive court-grade evidence within 24 hours.
- **SOC 2 Compliance**: Entire AINEFF deployment maintains continuous SOC 2 Type II compliance. Not annual audits -- continuous verification.
- **Escalation Workflows**: Billing leakage above $100K auto-escalates to CFO with 48hr SLA. Supply chain risk above threshold escalates to COO with 24hr SLA. Regulatory change with material impact escalates to General Counsel with 72hr SLA.

### AI-Native Coordination (43 Core Systems)
- **DocuFlow**: Processes 2.3M documents/month. Extracts, classifies, routes. Eliminates 34% document processing redundancy.
- **Billing Leakage Detector**: Continuous revenue assurance across all billing systems. Identifies patterns invisible to human auditors: systematic underbilling of service tiers, unauthorized discount chains, unbilled change orders.
- **Chokepoint Intelligence Engine**: Maps operational bottlenecks across the entire enterprise value chain. Quantifies cost of each chokepoint in dollars per day.
- **Multi-Model AI Orchestrator**: Routes AI inference across Claude/GPT/Gemini/open-source based on task requirements, cost optimization, and data sovereignty constraints. Eliminates vendor lock-in.
- **Enterprise Knowledge Graph**: Captures institutional knowledge from documents, communications, and operational data. Makes tribal knowledge searchable and survivable.
- **Board Decision Intelligence**: Replaces 500-page board decks with decision-ready briefings. Synthesizes from operational data, not management narrative.

### Audience-Specific Incentive Alignment
- **Operator Performance Analytics**: Objective measurement of human-AI collaboration effectiveness. Compensation linkable to measurable output, not activity metrics.
- **Internal Fraud Pattern Detector**: Identifies anomalous transaction patterns, expense report fraud, procurement kickbacks. Makes gaming the system computationally expensive.
- **Organizational Drift Detector**: Continuous measurement of behavioral alignment to stated corporate values. Makes culture measurable, not aspirational.

### Information Integrity Systems
- **Regulatory Change Tracker**: Monitors regulatory changes across all operating jurisdictions. Assesses impact within 24 hours of publication.
- **Supply Chain Risk Neural Network**: Models multi-tier supplier risk in real-time. Identifies cascading failure scenarios before they materialize.
- **M&A Due Diligence Accelerator**: Compresses months of due diligence into days. Analyzes targets across financial, legal, operational, and cultural dimensions simultaneously.

## E. ACCOUNTABILITY DESIGN

| Mechanism | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Single-point accountability (ETLB)** | Every AI-executed action binds to a named executive at moment of execution. Supply chain rerouting binds to COO. Revenue recognition adjustment binds to CFO. Product launch approval binds to CPO. No committee-level diffusion. One name, one decision, one binding. |
| **Decision rights (ORF)** | Corporate authority matrix encoded as ORF constraints. Budget approval limits, hiring authority, contract signing authority, regulatory filing authority -- all computationally enforced. Overrides require documented rationale and auto-trigger board notification above threshold. |
| **Escalation (E-AEGL)** | Tier 1: Automated operational alerts (billing anomalies, SLA breaches, policy violations) -- 24hr response SLA. Tier 2: Cross-functional impact events (supply chain disruption, regulatory change, security incident) -- 4hr response SLA. Tier 3: Material impact events (>$10M exposure, regulatory enforcement action, data breach) -- 1hr escalation to C-suite. |
| **Ratification layers (MCO)** | Every AI system deployment carries a 24-month mortality date. Every executive delegation of authority to an AI system expires. Every automated workflow must be re-ratified by the accountable human. Dead systems cannot self-resurrect. Authority cannot accumulate without human renewal. |
