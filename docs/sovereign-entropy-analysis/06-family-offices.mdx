## D. AINEFF DEPLOYMENT ARCHITECTURE

### ORF/ETLB/MCO Structural Constraints
- **ORF**: Every investment decision above $500K carries an Obligation Record binding the decision-maker (CIO or Principal) to a documented thesis, risk tolerance, and exit trigger. No commitment without finality.
- **ETLB**: At the moment of trade execution or capital commitment, the human authorizer is cryptographically bound to the decision. Advisor recommendations do not constitute authorization -- only the Principal or delegated CIO can trigger ETLB.
- **MCO**: Every investment thesis carries a mortality date. If the thesis has not been validated within its stated timeframe, the position enters mandatory review. No zombie allocations. PE/VC commitments carry MCO-enforced milestone checkpoints at 18, 36, and 60 months.

### E-AEGL Governance Hardening
- **Policy Enforcement**: Sub-10ms enforcement of investment policy statement constraints. Any transaction violating concentration limits, liquidity floors, or jurisdiction restrictions is blocked before execution.
- **SHA-256 Audit Chain**: Every advisor recommendation, family meeting decision, and portfolio rebalancing event is hash-chained. Court-grade evidence trail for succession disputes.
- **Escalation Workflows**: Advisor fee disputes auto-escalate to independent benchmarking review within 72 hours. G2 education milestones enforced with SLA-triggered notifications.

### AI-Native Coordination (43 Core Systems)
- **Alternative Investment Analyzer**: AI-driven due diligence on PE/VC/RE deal flow. Processes 500+ data points per opportunity vs. advisor's 20-30 manual checks.
- **Consolidated Reporting Platform**: Real-time multi-entity, multi-jurisdiction portfolio aggregation. Eliminates 45-90 day reporting lag.
- **Liquidity & Cash Flow Predictor**: Models liquidity needs across all holdings with capital call schedules, distribution forecasts, and currency exposure.
- **Tax-Efficient Structuring Advisor**: Multi-jurisdiction tax modeling. Replaces $200K+/year in advisor fees with continuous optimization.
- **Cybersecurity & Privacy Shield**: 24/7 digital footprint monitoring for all family members.

### Audience-Specific Incentive Alignment
- Advisor compensation benchmarked against industry data via **Vendor & Service Provider Optimizer**. Fee drag made visible and actionable.
- G2 education tied to portfolio exposure via **Next-Gen Education & Integration** -- learning paths generated from actual family holdings, not generic wealth management curricula.
- **Family Governance Facilitator**: Structured decision-making replacing informal family dynamics. Voting records, dissent documentation, minority position protection.

### Information Integrity Systems
- **Co-Investment Network Engine**: Broadens deal flow beyond advisor-controlled channels. AI-matched opportunities from 200+ family office networks.
- **ESG & Impact Scoring Engine**: Portfolio-level ESG scoring eliminates greenwashing by advisors marketing ESG products with higher fees but no measurable impact.

## E. ACCOUNTABILITY DESIGN

| Mechanism | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Single-point accountability (ETLB)** | Every capital commitment binds the authorizing human at execution time. No "the advisor recommended it" defense. Principal or CIO owns every entry and exit. |
| **Decision rights (ORF)** | Investment Policy Statement encoded as ORF constraints. Concentration limits, liquidity floors, jurisdiction restrictions, and ESG exclusions enforced computationally. Overrides require documented rationale with MCO-enforced review date. |
| **Escalation (E-AEGL)** | Tier 1: Automated alerts for policy threshold breaches (24hr response SLA). Tier 2: Advisor fee anomalies escalate to independent review (72hr). Tier 3: Succession governance gaps escalate to external trustee notification (7 days). |
| **Ratification layers (MCO)** | Every delegation of authority to an advisor, CIO, or family member carries an expiry date. Maximum delegation period: 24 months. Renewal requires documented performance review. Dead delegations cannot be resurrected -- new authorization required. |
