## 90-Day Deployment Roadmap

Ground-level execution plan. Every action is tied to a revenue outcome or a structural prerequisite for revenue. No architecture work that does not directly enable the next sale.

### Days 1-30: First Revenue or Death

**Objective:** Generate $15K-$75K from PIAR engagements. Prove that someone will pay for the intellectual property embedded in 535K lines of documentation.

| Day | Action | Deliverable | Revenue Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-3 | Identify 20 target CIOs/CTOs at mid-market enterprises (Aud 8, 9) and mid-tier consulting firms (Aud 12) | Prospect list with contact information, company AI spend, and pain point hypothesis | $0 |
| 4-7 | Draft PIAR sales deck: "We audit your AI investment architecture and identify governance gaps that will cost you money" | 15-slide deck, 2-page leave-behind, 1-page pricing sheet ($15K sprint / $35K standard / $75K comprehensive) | $0 |
| 8-14 | Cold outreach: 20 emails, 10 LinkedIn messages, 5 direct calls per day. Target: 5 discovery meetings booked | 5 confirmed meetings | $0 |
| 15-21 | Conduct discovery meetings. Deliver 3-page "quick assessment" free of charge. Close at least 1 paid PIAR engagement. | 1 signed SOW | $15K-$75K contracted |
| 22-30 | Deliver first PIAR report (30-50 pages). Use findings to identify upsell to Billing Leakage Detector or DocuFlow. | Delivered report + upsell proposal | $15K-$75K invoiced |

**Decision gate (Day 30):** If $0 contracted at Day 30, the sales motion is broken. Options: (a) change the target audience, (b) change the price, (c) change the value proposition, (d) accept that this is a consulting business, not a platform business, and pivot accordingly.

**Minimal AINEFF integration at this stage:** ETLB binding on the PIAR report itself (the deliverable is accountability-bound to the analyst). MCO on the assessment validity (12-month expiry on findings). This is not theater -- it is dog-fooding the protocol stack in the simplest possible context.

### Days 31-60: Revenue Repetition + First Product Pilot

**Objective:** Close PIAR engagement #2-3. Launch paid pilot for Billing Leakage Detector ($8K-$50K).

| Day | Action | Deliverable | Revenue Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 31-37 | Convert PIAR-1 upsell into Billing Leakage Detector pilot proposal. Simultaneously begin PIAR-2 delivery. | Pilot SOW for Billing Leakage Detector | $8K-$15K pilot |
| 38-45 | Build minimum viable Billing Leakage Detector: API consumption analyzer + billing reconciliation. Use PIAR-1 client's data. | Working prototype scanning live transaction data | $0 (building) |
| 46-52 | Run 7-day free leakage scan on PIAR-2 prospect's sample data. Show dollar amount of detected leakage. Close pilot. | Leakage report + conversion to paid pilot | $8K-$50K pilot |
| 53-60 | Begin DocuFlow prototype development in parallel (estimated 2-4 weeks). Close PIAR-3. | DocuFlow alpha + PIAR-3 SOW | $15K-$75K (PIAR-3) |

**Running revenue total at Day 60 (conservative):** $38K-$140K contracted.

**AINEFF integration at this stage:** Billing Leakage Detector outputs include ORF obligation tracking (each detected leakage is an obligation-with-finality -- the client must resolve it). ETLB binds the person who acknowledges or disputes each finding. MCO on the detection model (12-month expiry, re-validation required before renewal). This demonstrates protocol value without requiring enterprise-wide deployment.

### Days 61-90: Revenue Diversification + Governance Attachment Proof

**Objective:** Prove the Fries attach to the Burger. Achieve 40%+ governance attachment on existing clients. Launch Claims Processing Accelerator pilot.

| Day | Action | Deliverable | Revenue Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 61-70 | Convert 2+ existing clients to governance-layer subscription. Minimum: ETLB audit trail access + MCO fleet dashboard. | 2 governance subscriptions ($5K-$15K/month each) | $10K-$30K/month recurring |
| 71-80 | Target 1 mid-tier insurer (Aud 9) for Claims Processing Accelerator pilot. Leverage PIAR + Billing Leakage track record as credibility. | Signed pilot SOW | $15K-$75K |
| 81-85 | Launch AI Cost Optimization Engine as self-serve tool for Founders/Operators (Aud 14). Low touch, high volume, data moat contribution. | Public product page + first 10 sign-ups | $0-$5K (early adoption) |
| 86-90 | Quarter review. Calculate attachment rate across all clients. Model unit economics. Decide: platform or consulting? | Financial model with real data | Decision document |

**Running revenue total at Day 90 (conservative):** $75K-$300K contracted. $30K-$90K recurring monthly.

**Decision gate (Day 90):**
- If attachment rate &gt;= 40%: platform model validated. Invest in scaling.
- If attachment rate 20-39%: governance pricing or bundling needs adjustment. Do not scale until fixed.
- If attachment rate below 20%: Fries model is failing. Pivot to standalone governance or pure consulting.
- If total revenue below $50K: execution capacity is the binding constraint, not market demand. Consider co-founder or operator hire before doing anything else.

## 3-Year Structural Sovereignty Architecture

### Year 1: Revenue Proof (12 months)

**Target:** 50 customers. $900K-$1.2M revenue. 40%+ attachment rate. First regulator engagement.

| Quarter | Milestone | Revenue Target | Customer Count |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 (Months 1-3) | First revenue. PIAR + Billing Leakage. Dog-food protocols. | $75K-$300K | 5-15 |
| Q2 (Months 4-6) | Claims Accelerator launched. DocuFlow launched. First governance subscriptions. | $150K-$400K | 15-25 |
| Q3 (Months 7-9) | White-label governance SDK to 1-2 consulting firms (Aud 12). Regulatory Change Tracker pilot. | $200K-$500K | 25-35 |
| Q4 (Months 10-12) | Board Decision Intelligence launched. First regulator meeting (Singapore IMDA). Year-end review. | $250K-$600K | 35-50 |

**Structural milestones:**
- Governance-as-Default architecture operational (Moat 2)
- Industry Ontology v1.0 published (Moat 1)
- Kitchen data collection started (Moat 3 precursor)
- 1-2 operator hires from LevelUpMax pipeline
- ORF/ETLB/MCO specifications drafted for open publication

**Failure modes to watch:**
- Attachment rate below 30% at Q2 = bundling strategy needs emergency revision
- Zero consulting firm white-label partnerships at Q3 = distribution channel failing
- No regulator engagement at Q4 = standards capture timeline at risk
