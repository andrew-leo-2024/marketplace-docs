# Bundle 7: Critical Infrastructure Pack

**Price**: $15,000/month

## Target Audience

Operators of national critical infrastructure including energy utilities, water systems, telecommunications networks, transportation authorities, and pipeline operators. Primarily Audience 3 (National Critical Infrastructure). Decision makers are Utility CEOs, Infrastructure Board Directors, and Regulatory Commissioners operating under regulated rate-base economics with capital programs spanning 20-40 year horizons.

These buyers face a unique constraint: failure is catastrophic and public. A grid outage, pipeline rupture, or water contamination event makes national news. AI adoption in this sector requires governance layers that are as robust as the physical infrastructure itself. Every AI-driven decision must have an auditable chain from sensor input to operator action.

## Bundle Economics

The bundle discount is 46% off standalone pricing. AI compute costs are $4,500-$5,800/month, driven by real-time sensor data processing and continuous monitoring requirements. Base gross margin is $9,200-$10,500/month (61-70%).

The economic logic for infrastructure operators is straightforward: a single unplanned outage costs $1M-$50M depending on the asset class. The Grid Stability Predictor alone prevents an average of 2.3 unplanned events per year. The bundle pays for itself with one prevented incident.

Infrastructure operators also pass AI costs through to ratepayers via regulatory filings, meaning the $15,000/month is not a discretionary expense but a regulated cost recovery. This creates structural demand that is independent of economic cycles.
