# Audience Bundles

The FrankMax Marketplace packages its 713 tools into 7 audience-specific bundles. Each bundle is priced as a loss-leader (the "Burger") designed to land customers, with high-margin governance and compliance layers (the "Fries") attached on top.

## Bundle Economics

Every bundle operates on the Burger/Fries/Kitchen model:

- **Burger** (the bundle itself): Priced at or below cost of AI compute to acquire the customer
- **Fries** (attachment layers): Governance, compliance, audit, and certification layers at 70-95% margin
- **Kitchen** (compounding moat): Every transaction generates telemetry that feeds the Failure Pattern Library

The critical threshold is **40% attachment rate** -- below this, the bundle economics collapse. Current target: 60-70% attachment within 6 months of initial bundle sale.
