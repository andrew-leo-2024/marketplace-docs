# Bundle 4: Founder/Operator Sprint Pack

**Price**: $499/month

## Target Audience

Solo founders, small operators (1-20 employees), and bootstrapped startups deploying AI to replace headcount they cannot afford. Primarily Audience 14 (Founders, Solo Operators, and Micro-Enterprises). Decision makers are founders themselves, operating with personal or seed-stage capital, optimizing for speed-to-revenue over compliance.

This is the highest-volume, lowest-price bundle. It exists to capture the long tail of AI adopters who will graduate into enterprise bundles as they scale. The founder who pays $499/month today runs a 200-person company buying Bundle 3 in three years.

## Bundle Economics

At $499/month against $1,050 standalone, the discount is 52%. AI compute costs run $140-$200/month, yielding base gross margin of $299-$359/month (60-72%). On a per-customer basis, this is the lowest margin bundle. The economics only work at volume: 1,000 customers at $499 generates $499K MRR with $300K+ gross margin.

The strategic value is not in the base revenue. It is in three things: (1) deal flow intelligence from watching thousands of startups operate, (2) graduation revenue when founders scale, and (3) the referral network effect when founders recommend FrankMax to their investors, advisors, and portfolio peers.

**Weighted attachment revenue**: $229/month per customer. Combined with the $499 base, effective ARPU is $728/month. Attachment rates are the lowest of any bundle because founders optimize for cost, not compliance. Attachment increases sharply at Series A when board governance requirements kick in.

## Upgrade Path

- **Tier 2**: Add Team Coordination and Hiring Intelligence at $899/month
- **Growth Stage**: Migrate to Bundle 3 (Enterprise Operations Pack) at $4,500/month post-Series A
- **Vertical Specialization**: Founders in regulated industries add CoaaS and GaaS layers
- **Portfolio Play**: VC firms buy 10-50 seat licenses for portfolio companies at $399/seat/month

Founder contracts are month-to-month with no commitment. Churn is high (3-5% monthly) but acceptable because acquisition cost is near zero (organic, word-of-mouth, and content-driven). The critical metric is graduation rate: the percentage of founders who scale into higher-tier bundles within 24 months. Target is 8-12% graduation rate. Each customer generates byproduct data on startup failure patterns, market timing signals, and operator behavior that feeds Enterprise Mortality Tables and the Anti-Bullshit Filter.
