# Bundle 3: Enterprise Operations Pack

**Price**: $4,500/month

## Target Audience

Mid-market and enterprise companies (500-10,000 employees) deploying AI across operational functions. Primarily Audience 7 (Enterprise IT and Operations) and Audience 8 (HR, Talent, and Workforce Management). Decision makers are CIOs, CTOs, COOs, and VP-level operational leaders managing digital transformation budgets.

These buyers have already purchased AI models from providers but lack the orchestration, governance, and workflow layers to deploy them safely at scale. They are spending $20K-$100K/month on raw model access and getting 15-30% utilization. This bundle turns that waste into structured operational capability.

## Bundle Economics

The bundle discount is 49% off standalone pricing. AI compute costs run $1,200-$1,600/month, yielding base gross margin of $2,900-$3,300/month (64-73%). The Cost Optimization Engine alone typically identifies $5,000-$15,000/month in wasted AI spend within 30 days, making the bundle self-funding from day one.

Enterprise operations is the highest-volume bundle by customer count. While the per-customer revenue is lower than financial services or defense, the addressable market is 50x larger. Volume economics drive this bundle.
