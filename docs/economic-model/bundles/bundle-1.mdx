# Bundle 1: Government Starter Pack

**Price**: $2,500/month

## Target Audience

Government agencies, ministries, and municipal bodies entering AI adoption for the first time. Primarily Audience 1 (Governments and Ministries) and Audience 4 (Public Sector Adjacent organizations including NGOs, multilateral bodies, and public-interest institutions). Decision makers are Permanent Secretaries, Directors-General, and IT Directors operating within annual appropriation cycles.

This bundle is designed for agencies that need to demonstrate responsible AI adoption to oversight bodies before scaling. It solves the "we want to try AI but procurement takes 18 months" problem by packaging pre-governed tools at a price point that fits under discretionary spending thresholds.

## Bundle Economics

The standalone total for these six tools is $5,700/month. At $2,500/month, the bundle represents a 56% discount, making it the entry-point loss leader (the Burger). The AI model compute cost to serve this bundle is approximately $800-$1,100/month, leaving $1,400-$1,700 in gross margin on the base bundle alone. However, the real economics come from attachment.

Government buyers who start with the Starter Pack generate 2.3x the attachment revenue within 6 months because compliance requirements cascade: one AI deployment triggers audit obligations, which trigger governance requirements, which trigger reporting obligations.
