# Bundle 5: Dynasty/Family Office Continuity Pack

**Price**: $12,000/month

## Target Audience

Ultra-high-net-worth families, single-family offices, multi-family offices, and dynasty-scale wealth management operations. Primarily Audience 5 (Family Offices and Wealth Management) and Audience 6 (Estate, Trust, and Succession Planning). Decision makers are family principals, family office CEOs, trust attorneys, and wealth advisors managing generational wealth transfer across 2-5 generations.

These buyers face a unique problem: the decisions they make today affect beneficiaries who will not be born for 30 years. Standard enterprise software has a 3-5 year planning horizon. This bundle operates on a 50-100 year planning horizon, which no other product in market addresses.

## Bundle Economics

The bundle discount is 42% off standalone pricing. AI compute costs are $3,200-$4,000/month, yielding base gross margin of $8,000-$8,800/month (67-73%). The MCO Engine is the strategic anchor: it creates a machine-readable mortality-aware plan that binds obligations across generations, making the platform structurally irreplaceable once activated.

The economics are asymmetric. A single family office managing $500M+ in assets pays $144,000/year for this bundle. The alternative is a team of 3-5 estate attorneys, tax advisors, and family governance consultants costing $800K-$1.5M/year for inferior coverage.
