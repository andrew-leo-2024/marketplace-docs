# Bundle 2: Financial Services Compliance Pack

**Price**: $8,500/month

## Target Audience

Regulated financial institutions including banks, insurance companies, asset managers, broker-dealers, and fintech companies operating under prudential oversight. Primarily Audience 9 (Financial Services and Fintech). Decision makers are Chief Compliance Officers, Chief Risk Officers, and Heads of Regulatory Affairs operating under continuous examination cycles from regulators (OCC, FDIC, SEC, FCA, MAS, OSFI).

Financial services firms face the highest density of AI compliance obligations of any sector. Every model used in credit decisioning, fraud detection, or customer-facing applications requires documentation, validation, and ongoing monitoring. This bundle makes that affordable.

## Bundle Economics

At $8,500/month against $15,400 standalone, the bundle discount is 45%. AI compute costs run $2,800-$3,400/month for this bundle due to the complexity of financial models and real-time transaction monitoring. Base gross margin is $5,100-$5,700/month (60-67%).

The critical economics: a single regulatory examination finding related to AI governance costs a financial institution $500K-$5M in remediation. This bundle's annual cost of $102,000 is a rounding error against that risk, making the sale rational on pure loss-avoidance math.
