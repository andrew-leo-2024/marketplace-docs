# Bundle 6: Defense & Intelligence Pack

**Price**: $25,000/month

## Target Audience

Defense ministries, intelligence agencies, military branches, and defense contractors operating in classified and sensitive environments. Primarily Audience 2 (Defense, Security, and Intelligence). Decision makers are Flag Officers, Agency Directors, Defense Secretaries, and Program Managers operating under classified budgets, multi-year defense appropriations, and stringent security requirements (ITAR, EAR, security clearance mandates).

These buyers cannot use commercial AI products without governance wrappers that enforce classification boundaries, audit chains, and human-in-the-loop kill switches. The FrankMax Execution-Time Liability Binding (ETLB) protocol was designed specifically for this constraint: every AI action binds to a named human with clearance-appropriate authority.

## Bundle Economics

The bundle discount is 41% off standalone. AI compute costs are higher than other bundles at $8,000-$10,000/month due to the complexity of multi-source fusion, scenario modeling, and the requirement for isolated compute environments. Base gross margin is $15,000-$17,000/month (60-68%).

Defense economics are driven by contract size, not margin percentage. A single five-year defense contract at $25,000/month generates $1.5M in base revenue. With attachments, that number exceeds $3M per contract. The sales cycle is 6-18 months, but contract duration and renewal rates make this the highest lifetime value segment.
