# Regulatory Pre-Compliance Sandboxes

## What It Is

Regulatory Pre-Compliance Sandboxes are the byproduct of the platform's compliance and certification engines continuously mapping AI deployments against regulatory requirements. As the platform processes compliance for thousands of customers, it builds a comprehensive model of every existing and emerging AI regulation worldwide. This model powers sandboxes where organizations can test their AI deployments against regulations that have not yet taken effect -- compliance with tomorrow's rules using today's infrastructure.

Pre-compliance sandboxes solve a problem that costs organizations billions annually: regulatory surprise. When a new regulation is announced with a 24-month implementation window, organizations scramble to assess impact and build compliance programs. With pre-compliance sandboxes, organizations can test their current AI deployments against draft regulations in hours, identify gaps immediately, and begin remediation before the regulation is even finalized. The platform can offer this because it has already mapped the compliance landscape for thousands of similar deployments.

## How It's Generated

The compliance engine generates sandbox data from:

- Regulatory framework models: machine-readable representations of every AI-relevant regulation
- Draft regulation tracking: automated parsing of proposed regulations, consultation papers, and guidance documents
- Cross-jurisdictional mapping: how regulations in one jurisdiction predict requirements in others
- Compliance gap patterns: which types of AI deployments consistently fail which types of regulatory requirements
- Remediation playbooks: what changes organizations make to achieve compliance and how long they take
- Regulatory evolution data: how draft regulations change between proposal and enactment

- Legal teams assessing the cost and scope of upcoming compliance obligations
- Government relations teams advising regulators on the practical impact of proposed rules
- Regulators themselves wanting to understand how proposed rules affect real AI deployments
- Industry associations preparing comment letters on proposed regulations

## Compounding Effect

Pre-compliance sandbox accuracy improves with every regulation that transitions from proposal to enforcement. Each completed regulatory cycle teaches the platform how draft language translates to actual compliance requirements. At 10 completed cycles, the platform can predict final requirements from draft text with 60% accuracy. At 100 completed cycles, accuracy exceeds 85%. This predictive capability becomes a moat that grows wider with every regulatory cycle, because the training data can only be generated by operating a compliance platform through multiple regulatory transitions.
