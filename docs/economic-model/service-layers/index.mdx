# As-a-Service Layers

The 15 as-a-service layers are the "Fries" of the Burger/Fries/Kitchen economic model. They attach to every bundle and tool as high-margin (70-95%) governance, compliance, and operational layers. These layers are where the marketplace generates profit -- the tools themselves (the Burger) are loss-leaders.

## Attachment Economics

The 40% attachment rate threshold is the survival line. Below 40%, the marketplace cannot sustain the loss-leader pricing of the Burger (tools). Above 60%, the economics become self-reinforcing -- each service layer creates dependency that increases retention and enables further attachment.
