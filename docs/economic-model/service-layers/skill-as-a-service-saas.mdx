# Skill-as-a-Service (SaaS)

## Definition

Skill-as-a-Service (SaaS) delivers composite AI skills -- multi-step sequences of capabilities chained together to perform a complete business task. Where CaaS provides "extract data from this document," SaaS provides "process this insurance claim end-to-end: extract data, validate against policy terms, check for fraud indicators, calculate payout, and draft the decision letter."

SaaS represents the second rung on the Fries ladder. It bundles multiple CaaS invocations into a single skill execution with its own governance wrapper, error handling, and human escalation points. Each skill is domain-specific, pre-validated for accuracy, and priced at a premium over the sum of its component capabilities because the orchestration logic itself is the value.

## How It Works

1. Customer invokes a skill from the catalog (e.g., "Process Insurance Claim")
2. The skill engine decomposes the task into ordered capability steps
3. Each step executes with its own governance check and output validation
4. Human escalation triggers if confidence drops below configured thresholds
5. Final output aggregated, formatted, and delivered with full step-by-step audit trail
6. Skill performance data feeds pattern optimization in the Kitchen layer

## Target Audiences

- **Primary**: Audience 7 (Enterprise IT), Audience 8 (HR/Talent), Audience 14 (Founders)
- **Secondary**: Audience 9 (Financial Services), Audience 10 (Healthcare)
- **Attach Rate**: Highest with Bundle 3 (Enterprise Operations) and Bundle 4 (Founder Sprint)

## Pricing Model

- **Per-execution**: $2-$50 per skill execution depending on complexity
- **Subscription**: $150-$900/month for unlimited executions of specific skill packs
- **Custom skills**: $5,000 one-time build fee + $500/month for custom skill development
- **Enterprise volume**: Negotiated rates at 1,000+ executions/month

## Revenue Economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Gross Margin | 72-85% |
| AI Compute Cost | 12-22% of execution price |
| Orchestration Overhead | 3-6% |
| Average Monthly Revenue per Customer | $150-$3,200 |
| Margin Expansion Trigger | Skill reuse across customers amortizes build cost |

SaaS margins are structurally higher than CaaS because the orchestration logic is built once and sold thousands of times. A skill that costs $15,000 to develop and validate generates $150K+ in annual revenue across 50 customers.
