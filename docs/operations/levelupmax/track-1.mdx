# Track 1: AI-Native Operator Foundation

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Price** | $900 -- $1,500 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based bootcamp (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Early-career professionals (0--5 years experience) |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax AI-Native Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for any 30-day specialist track (Tracks 2--4, 6--7, 9--10) |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can deconstruct any business workflow, identify where AI creates leverage, and redesign the process for AI-native execution. They are not AI enthusiasts -- they are operators who understand how AI changes the structure of work.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Workflow Deconstruction
- Breaking business processes into decision nodes, information flows, and action steps
- Identifying human bottlenecks, judgment points, and automation candidates
- Mapping process dependencies and failure propagation paths

### Week 2: AI Capability Mapping
- Understanding what current AI models can and cannot do (Claude, GPT, Gemini, open-source)
- Matching AI capabilities to workflow nodes: classification, generation, extraction, reasoning, orchestration
- Cost-performance tradeoffs across model providers and deployment patterns

### Week 3: AI-Native Process Redesign
- Redesigning workflows from first principles with AI as a core operator, not a bolt-on
- Designing human-AI handoff points with appropriate oversight
- Building governance checkpoints into redesigned workflows

### Week 4: Capstone Project
- Select a real business process from participant's organization
- Produce a complete workflow deconstruction + AI-native redesign document
- Present to cohort with peer review and instructor assessment

## Advancement Path

Track 1 is the foundation. After completion, operators choose a specialization:

- **Track 2**: Governance & Risk Operator -- for compliance/risk career paths
- **Track 3**: Revenue Systems Operator -- for sales ops/revenue management paths
- **Track 4**: Capital Allocation & Portfolio Operator -- for finance career paths
- **Track 6**: Data Infrastructure Operator -- for data engineering paths
- **Track 7**: Enterprise Workflow Optimization Operator -- for operations management paths
- **Track 9**: Compliance & Regulatory Deployment Operator -- for legal/compliance paths
- **Track 10**: Performance Intelligence & Learning Systems Operator -- for L&D/OD paths

Or proceed directly to an advanced track:

- **Track 5**: Venture Production Operator (90 days) -- for aspiring founders
- **Track 8**: AI Engineering & Systems Architect (90 days) -- for engineers
