# Track 7: Enterprise Workflow Optimization Operator

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Price** | $1,200 -- $1,800 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based bootcamp (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Operations managers, process improvement specialists, lean/six sigma practitioners |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax Enterprise Workflow Optimization Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for Track 5 (Venture Production) or enterprise deployment roles |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can apply process mining techniques to identify operational bottlenecks, design AI-automated workflows that replace manual handoffs, measure optimization impact in financial terms, and operate continuous improvement systems powered by AI telemetry. They convert operational waste into measurable savings.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Process Mining & Bottleneck Identification
- Process mining techniques: extracting workflow patterns from system logs and transaction data
- Bottleneck taxonomy: approval delays, handoff failures, rework loops, information gaps
- Quantifying bottleneck cost: translating operational delays into financial impact

### Week 2: AI-Powered Workflow Automation
- Identifying automation candidates: which workflow steps benefit from AI vs. RPA vs. human judgment
- Designing human-AI handoff points: when AI decides, when AI recommends, when AI assists
- Building governance into automated workflows: audit trails, override capabilities, escalation triggers

### Week 3: Optimization Measurement & Continuous Improvement
- Defining optimization metrics that connect to financial outcomes (not vanity metrics)
- A/B testing workflow changes in production environments
- Continuous improvement loops: AI telemetry feeding back into process redesign

### Week 4: Capstone Project
- Analyze a simulated enterprise workflow using process mining techniques
- Identify top 5 optimization opportunities with financial impact quantification
- Design AI-automated replacements with governance integration
- Present ROI analysis

## Advancement Path

After Track 7, operators can:

- Proceed to **Track 5: Venture Production Operator** (90 days) to optimize workflows within a venture cell
- Deploy directly into enterprise clients as workflow optimization operators
- Enter the **Venture Cell Sandbox** with enterprise operations role
