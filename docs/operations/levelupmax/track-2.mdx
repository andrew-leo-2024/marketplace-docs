# Track 2: Governance & Risk Operator

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Price** | $1,200 -- $1,800 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based bootcamp (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Compliance officers, risk managers, internal auditors, GRC professionals |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax Governance & Risk Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for Track 5 (Venture Production) or Track 9 (Compliance & Regulatory) |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can operate AI governance systems, enforce compliance policies through automated kill-switches, manage risk registers that are operationalized (not decorative), and structure accountability chains for AI-driven decisions.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: AI Governance Foundations
- Regulatory landscape: EU AI Act, US state-level AI laws, Singapore AIDA, emerging GCC/ASEAN frameworks
- Governance architecture: policies, controls, enforcement mechanisms, audit trails
- The ETLB (Execution-Time Liability Binding) and MCO (Mortality Compliance Object) protocols

### Week 2: Policy Enforcement & Kill-Switch Management
- Designing automated policy enforcement (not policy documents -- enforcement mechanisms)
- Kill-switch architecture: when to halt AI operations, who authorizes resumption, how to document
- Escalation path design: automated detection to human intervention to board notification

### Week 3: Risk Operationalization
- Converting static risk registers into live monitoring systems
- Designing risk metrics that trigger action (not risk metrics that populate reports)
- Failure propagation modeling: how one risk event cascades through governance structures

### Week 4: Capstone Project
- Design a complete AI governance framework for a simulated enterprise deployment
- Include policy enforcement mechanisms, kill-switch criteria, escalation paths, and audit architecture
- Present with adversarial review (instructor plays the regulator)

## Advancement Path

After Track 2, operators can:

- Proceed to **Track 5: Venture Production Operator** (90 days) to build governance-native venture cells
- Proceed to **Track 9: Compliance & Regulatory Deployment Operator** for deeper regulatory specialization
- Enter the **Venture Cell Sandbox** with governance operator role
