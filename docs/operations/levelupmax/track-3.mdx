# Track 3: Revenue Systems Operator

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Price** | $1,200 -- $1,800 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based bootcamp (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Sales operations managers, revenue managers, billing analysts, finance operations |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax Revenue Systems Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for Track 4 (Capital Allocation) or Track 5 (Venture Production) |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can identify and quantify revenue leakage, design AI-powered billing optimization systems, structure revenue assurance workflows, and operate the tools that prevent margin erosion. They see revenue systems as engineered structures, not spreadsheet exercises.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Revenue Leakage Detection
- Taxonomy of revenue leakage: billing errors, unauthorized discounting, contract non-enforcement, pricing inconsistencies
- Quantification methods: estimating leakage as percentage of revenue by category
- AI-powered pattern detection: identifying leakage signals in transaction data

### Week 2: Billing Optimization & Assurance
- Billing system architecture: how charges flow from service delivery to invoice to collection
- Error detection patterns: duplicate charges, missed charges, rate misapplication, contract-price divergence
- Designing automated reconciliation workflows

### Week 3: Pricing Governance & Margin Protection
- Discount governance: who can authorize what discount, under what conditions, with what documentation
- Price consistency across channels, geographies, and customer segments
- Margin visibility dashboards: real-time margin tracking by product, customer, and deal

### Week 4: Capstone Project
- Analyze a simulated revenue dataset to identify leakage patterns and quantify total exposure
- Design a revenue assurance system with automated detection, alert routing, and remediation workflows
- Present findings with financial impact analysis

| Component | Weight |

| Peer review participation | 10% |

Minimum passing score: 70%. Certificate issued upon completion.

## Advancement Path

After Track 3, operators can:

- Proceed to **Track 4: Capital Allocation & Portfolio Operator** for finance-focused advancement
- Proceed to **Track 5: Venture Production Operator** (90 days) to run revenue systems in a venture cell
- Enter the **Venture Cell Sandbox** with revenue operations role
