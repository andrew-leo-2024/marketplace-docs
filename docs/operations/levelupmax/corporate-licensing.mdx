# Corporate Licensing Model

LevelUpMax offers enterprise licensing for organizations that want to develop AI-native operator capability at scale across their workforce.

---

## Licensing Packages

| Package | Price | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Single Track License** | $1,200 -- $2,200 per employee | Access to one track for one employee; includes all materials, cohort participation, assessment, and certification |
| **Team License (5--10)** | $800 -- $1,500 per person | Volume discount for team enrollment in a single track; dedicated team cohort available at 10+ |
| **Enterprise License** | $50,000 -- $150,000/year | Unlimited access to all 10 tracks for the organization; custom content modules; dedicated cohort scheduling; quarterly capability assessments |
| **Train-the-Trainer** | $25,000 per engagement | Certify internal trainers to deliver LevelUpMax content within the organization; includes instructor certification, materials license, and quality assurance framework |

---

## Enterprise License Details

The Enterprise License is the full-access package for organizations committed to building AI-native capability across their workforce.

### What's Included

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| **All 10 Tracks** | Unlimited employee enrollment across all tracks for the license period |
| **Custom Content Modules** | Industry-specific or organization-specific modules developed in collaboration |
| **Dedicated Cohort Scheduling** | Run cohorts on the organization's timeline, not the public schedule |
| **Quarterly Capability Assessments** | Organization-wide measurement of AI-native capability development |
| **Executive Briefings** | Quarterly briefing for leadership on capability development progress and ROI |
| **Priority Advancement** | Fast-track access to Venture Cell Sandbox for top performers |

### Enterprise License Pricing

| Organization Size | Annual License Fee |
|---|---|
| Up to 100 employees | $50,000/year |
| 100--500 employees | $75,000 -- $100,000/year |
| 500+ employees | $100,000 -- $150,000/year |

## Train-the-Trainer Program

For organizations that want to internalize delivery:

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 2-week intensive + 4-week supervised delivery |
| **Trainer Requirements** | Completed at least 2 LevelUpMax tracks; 3+ years industry experience |
| **Certification** | LevelUpMax Certified Instructor designation |
| **Materials License** | 2-year renewable license for all track materials |
| **Quality Assurance** | Annual instructor re-certification and materials update |

## Revenue Model: Cohort Economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Participants per cohort | 25 |
| Cohorts per year | 4 |
| Average revenue per cohort | $41,250 |
| Average cost per cohort (instruction, materials, platform) | $18,000 |
| **Margin per cohort** | **$23,250 (56%)** |
| Annual revenue (4 cohorts, single track) | $165,000 |
| Annual margin (4 cohorts, single track) | $93,000 |

At 10 tracks running 4 cohorts each = 40 cohorts/year = $1,650,000 revenue, $930,000 margin.

Enterprise licenses add recurring revenue above cohort economics.
