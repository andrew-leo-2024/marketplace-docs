# Track 4: Capital Allocation & Portfolio Operator

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 30 days |
| **Price** | $1,500 -- $2,200 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based bootcamp (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Finance professionals, FP&A analysts, portfolio managers, investment analysts |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax Capital Allocation Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for Track 5 (Venture Production) |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can manage capital allocation using AI-powered decision frameworks, operate milestone-gated investment processes, design capital envelope structures that prevent over-commitment, and monitor portfolio performance with real-time intelligence. They treat capital allocation as a system, not a budget cycle.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Capital Envelope Management
- Capital envelope design: defining allocation boundaries by business unit, project, and time horizon
- Constraint architecture: hard limits, soft limits, escalation triggers, override protocols
- AI-powered allocation modeling: scenario analysis across multiple capital deployment strategies

### Week 2: Milestone Gating & Investment Governance
- Milestone-gated capital release: defining gates, approval criteria, and evidence requirements
- Kill criteria: when to halt investment, who decides, what evidence triggers the decision
- Transition triggers: when to accelerate investment based on performance data

### Week 3: Portfolio Intelligence
- Real-time portfolio performance monitoring across multiple asset classes and ventures
- Risk correlation analysis: identifying hidden portfolio concentration risks
- AI-powered scenario modeling: stress-testing portfolios against macro and micro events

### Week 4: Capstone Project
- Design a capital allocation framework for a simulated multi-venture portfolio
- Include envelope structures, milestone gates, kill criteria, and monitoring dashboards
- Present with adversarial review (instructor challenges allocation decisions)

| Component | Weight |

## Advancement Path

After Track 4, operators can:

- Proceed to **Track 5: Venture Production Operator** (90 days) to manage capital allocation within a venture cell
- Enter the **Venture Cell Sandbox** with capital allocation operator role
- Advance toward portfolio management roles within the FrankMax ecosystem
