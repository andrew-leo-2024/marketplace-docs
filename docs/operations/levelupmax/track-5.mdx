# Track 5: Venture Production Operator

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 90 days |
| **Price** | $2,500 -- $3,500 |
| **Format** | Cohort-based intensive (25 participants max) |
| **Target Participant** | Aspiring founders, intrapreneurs, venture operators |
| **Output** | LevelUpMax Venture Production Operator Certificate |
| **Advancement** | Qualifies for Venture Cell Sandbox (90-day live deployment) |

---

## What This Track Produces

Graduates can create, launch, and operate a complete venture cell from zero. They understand every layer: market identification, product architecture, governance integration, capital management, revenue operations, and scaling mechanics. This is not entrepreneurship theory -- it is production-grade venture assembly.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Month 1: Venture Architecture
- **Week 1**: Market selection using chokepoint analysis and NAICS/SIC mapping
- **Week 2**: Product architecture -- designing the minimum viable system (not MVP -- minimum viable *system*)
- **Week 3**: Governance integration -- embedding ETLB, MCO, and ORF protocols from day one
- **Week 4**: Capital structure design -- envelope management, milestone gates, kill criteria

### Month 2: Venture Assembly
- **Week 5**: Revenue system design -- pricing, billing, leakage prevention, margin monitoring
- **Week 6**: Operations architecture -- workflows, handoffs, escalation paths, SLAs
- **Week 7**: Technology stack selection -- multi-model orchestration, infrastructure, deployment
- **Week 8**: Team structure and operator roles -- who does what, under what authority, with what accountability

### Month 3: Venture Execution
- **Week 9**: Go-to-market execution -- first customer acquisition, sales process, delivery mechanics
- **Week 10**: Performance measurement -- metrics that matter, metrics that deceive, kill-switch triggers
- **Week 11**: Scaling readiness assessment -- what must be true before scaling, what breaks at scale
- **Week 12**: Capstone: Launch a simulated venture cell with full governance, operations, and revenue architecture

## Assessment & Certification

| Component | Weight |
|---|---|
| Monthly milestone deliverables | 30% |
| Peer collaboration and review | 10% |
| Capstone venture cell design | 40% |
| Live pitch and adversarial review | 20% |

Minimum passing score: 75% (higher bar for advanced track). Certificate issued upon completion.

---

## Advancement Path

Track 5 is the gateway to live production:

1. **Venture Cell Sandbox** (90 days) -- deploy a real venture cell with limited capital and governance oversight
2. **Production** -- graduate from sandbox to full production with revenue targets
3. **Intrapreneurship** -- run a venture cell within an enterprise client's organization
4. **Equity** -- earn equity participation based on venture cell performance
5. **Spinout** -- spin the venture cell into an independent entity
