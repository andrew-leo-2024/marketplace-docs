## Strategic Analysis

### Revenue Concentration
Chokepoints 1, 7, 8, 17, and 25 collectively represent the highest monthly cost of inaction -- ranging from $100K to $10M per month. These are the first tools to build because the buyer already has budget authority and measurable pain.

### Cross-Industry Breadth
Chokepoints 2, 4, 7, 22, and 23 apply to "All" or "All regulated" NAICS sectors. These horizontal tools have the widest addressable market and justify platform investment.

### Compliance-Driven Urgency
Chokepoints 2, 4, 9, 10, 17, 18, 21, and 22 are driven by regulatory enforcement. The buyer does not choose to address these -- the regulator forces them. This creates non-optional purchasing behavior.

### Insurance Integration Opportunities
Chokepoints 7, 8, 17, and 25 directly affect insurability. As cyber and AI liability insurance tightens underwriting requirements, tools addressing these chokepoints become conditions of coverage.
