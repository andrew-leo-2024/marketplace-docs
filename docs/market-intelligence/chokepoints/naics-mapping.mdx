## Industry Coverage Analysis

### Sectors by Audience Concentration

**Audience 7 (Multinational Corporates)** appears as a primary audience in 13 of 20 sectors -- the broadest reach of any audience segment. Enterprise sales targeting multinationals have the widest NAICS coverage.

**Audience 8 (Legacy Enterprises)** appears in 11 of 20 sectors. These are mid-market organizations with established operations and immediate AI adoption needs.

**Audiences 1 and 3 (Government and Critical Infrastructure)** concentrate in regulated, infrastructure-heavy sectors (221, 481-488, 921-928). Sales cycles are longer but deal sizes are larger.
