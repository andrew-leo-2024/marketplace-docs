## Entry Product Bundle

The construction wedge enters with three tools that address the industry's most expensive operational failures:

| Tool | Chokepoint Addressed | Typical Monthly Cost of Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Project Risk Predictor** | Cost overruns, schedule delays (NAICS primary problem) | $25K--$500K |
| **Safety Compliance Monitor** | Safety compliance violations (Chokepoint #19) | $10K--$1M |
| **Job Costing Intelligence** | Job costing inaccuracy (Chokepoint #32) | $15K--$200K |

Combined, these three tools address $50K--$1.7M in monthly cost-of-inaction. At a $90K entry price, the buyer achieves ROI within 45--60 days.

## Why Construction in Phase 4

Construction enters in Phase 4 as a cross-industry proof point:

1. **Physical + regulatory** -- Construction combines physical operations (like manufacturing) with heavy regulatory compliance (like banking). Proving the marketplace works here demonstrates cross-domain capability.
2. **Project-based economics** -- Construction projects have defined budgets, timelines, and scope. AI impact is measurable per-project, making ROI clear and attributable.
3. **Safety economics** -- A single safety incident can cost $500K--$5M (OSHA fines, litigation, insurance, project delays). Safety compliance tools have extreme ROI.
4. **Industry digitization gap** -- Construction is one of the least digitized industries globally. The opportunity to be the first credible AI platform in construction creates category-defining positioning.

## Sales Process

### Week 1--2: Discovery & Qualification
- Identify projects with highest cost or schedule variance
- Review safety incident history and OSHA citation record
- Map job costing process: current accuracy, variance patterns, root causes

### Week 3--4: Pilot Deployment
- Deploy Project Risk Predictor on one active project (connect to project management, scheduling, and cost data)
- Activate Safety Compliance Monitor on one job site
- Integrate Job Costing Intelligence with existing accounting/ERP system

### Week 5--6: ROI Measurement
- Measure risk predictions against actual outcomes (cost forecasting accuracy)
- Track safety compliance improvement (violations identified before incidents)
- Quantify job costing accuracy improvement
- Produce per-project ROI report

### Week 7--8: Expansion Proposal
- Propose deployment across all active projects
- Bundle additional tools (Supplier Compliance Verifier, Proposal Generation Engine)
- Structure annual contract with governance layer attachment
