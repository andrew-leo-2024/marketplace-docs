## Entry Product Bundle

The professional services wedge enters with three tools that attack the structural economics of knowledge work:

| Tool | Chokepoint Addressed | Typical Monthly Cost of Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Knowledge Reuse Engine** | Institutional knowledge loss, re-creation of existing work (Chokepoint #54) | $20K--$300K |
| **Engagement Scoper** | Scope creep, out-of-scope commitments (Chokepoint #63) | $15K--$200K |
| **Due Diligence Suite** | Manual due diligence bottlenecks, inconsistent quality | $10K--$150K |

Combined, these three tools address $45K--$650K in monthly cost-of-inaction. At a $110K entry price, the buyer achieves ROI within 60--90 days.

---

## Why Professional Services in Phase 3

Professional services enters in Phase 3 after physical-operations proof (manufacturing + logistics):

1. **Knowledge work proof** -- After proving AI saves money in factories and logistics, professional services proves AI creates value in knowledge-intensive environments. This opens the entire white-collar market.
2. **High ARPA** -- Professional services firms have high average revenue per account. The $110K entry price is modest relative to firm revenue.
3. **Partner economics** -- Consulting firms and law firms measure everything in billable hours. AI that recovers 10% of wasted time on re-creating existing knowledge translates directly to revenue capacity.
4. **Multiplier effect** -- Consulting firms (Audience 12) deploy AI internally, then recommend it to their clients. One professional services sale creates multiple downstream referrals.

## Sales Process

### Week 1--3: Discovery & Qualification
- Identify practice groups with highest scope variance or knowledge re-creation rates
- Quantify knowledge loss: hours spent recreating deliverables that exist somewhere in the firm
- Map scope creep patterns: frequency, financial impact, root causes

### Week 4--6: Pilot Deployment
- Deploy Knowledge Reuse on target practice group's document library
- Connect Engagement Scoper to project management and time-tracking systems
- Configure Due Diligence Suite for target deal types

### Week 7--9: ROI Measurement
- Measure time savings from knowledge reuse (hours recovered per professional per week)
- Track scope adherence improvement (proposals vs. actuals)
- Quantify due diligence cycle time reduction
- Produce ROI report in partner-friendly language (recovered billable hours, not technical metrics)

### Week 10--12: Expansion Proposal
- Propose firm-wide deployment based on practice group results
- Bundle additional tools (Proposal Generation Engine, Contract Intelligence Platform)
- Structure annual contract with governance layer attachment
