## Entry Product Bundle

The logistics wedge enters with three tools that address the most expensive operational inefficiencies:

| Tool | Chokepoint Addressed | Typical Monthly Cost of Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Route Optimization AI** | Inefficient routing and logistics (Chokepoint #39) | $15K--$200K |
| **Fleet Maintenance Predictor** | Unplanned vehicle downtime (related to Chokepoint #15) | $10K--$150K |
| **Shipment Integrity Monitor** | Shipment loss and damage (Chokepoint #16) | $10K--$100K |

Combined, these three tools address $35K--$450K in monthly cost-of-inaction. At an $80K entry price, the buyer achieves ROI within 30--45 days.

---

## Why Logistics in Phase 2

Logistics scales the marketplace to physical operations alongside manufacturing:

1. **Speed of proof** -- Route optimization produces measurable savings (fuel, time, labor) within days, not weeks. Fastest ROI demonstration of any vertical.
2. **High data velocity** -- GPS, telematics, shipment tracking, and warehouse management systems generate continuous data streams. This feeds the marketplace's telemetry layer.
3. **Network effects** -- Logistics connects manufacturers, retailers, and distributors. One logistics client creates touchpoints across multiple verticals.
4. **Short sales cycle** -- 30--45 days is the shortest sales cycle of any wedge. Logistics operators make fast decisions because delay costs are visible daily.

## Sales Process

### Week 1--2: Discovery & Deployment
- Identify highest-cost routes or most underutilized fleet segments
- Connect Route Optimization to existing TMS/GPS data
- Deploy Fleet Maintenance Predictor on target vehicles
- Begin Shipment Integrity monitoring on highest-loss corridors

### Week 3--4: ROI Measurement
- Measure fuel savings, route time reduction, maintenance prediction accuracy
- Track damage reduction and loss prevention rates
- Produce weekly ROI snapshots (logistics buyers expect fast feedback loops)

### Week 5--6: Expansion & Contract
- Present full ROI analysis to Head of Logistics
- Propose network-wide deployment
- Structure annual contract with governance layer attachment
