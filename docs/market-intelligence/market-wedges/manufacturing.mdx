## Entry Product Bundle

The manufacturing wedge enters with three tools that address the highest-cost operational chokepoints:

| Tool | Chokepoint Addressed | Typical Monthly Cost of Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Predictive Maintenance Platform** | Unplanned production downtime (Chokepoint #15) | $50K--$2M |
| **Quality Prediction Engine** | Defects, rework, scrap (NAICS primary problem) | $25K--$500K |
| **Supply Chain Risk Monitor** | Supplier dependency, lead-time variability (Chokepoints #24, #41) | $10K--$200K |

Combined, these three tools address $85K--$2.7M in monthly cost-of-inaction. At a $95K entry price, the buyer achieves ROI within 30--60 days.

---

## Why Manufacturing First (Phase 2)

Manufacturing is the second wedge (after Insurance Claims in Phase 1) because:

1. **Physical world proof** -- AI saving money on a factory floor is tangible and visible. It proves AI works beyond digital processes.
2. **Data density** -- Manufacturing generates massive sensor, quality, and production data. This data feeds the marketplace's failure intelligence library.
3. **Cluster effects** -- Manufacturers talk to each other through industry associations (Audience 10) and supply chain relationships. One deployment creates referral pressure.
4. **Chokepoint concentration** -- Chokepoints #15, #19, #20, #24, #28, #29, #41, and #47 all cluster in manufacturing. Each entry opens upsell paths.

## Sales Process

### Week 1--2: Discovery & Qualification
- Identify target plants with highest downtime or defect costs
- Quantify current cost-of-inaction using buyer's own data (maintenance logs, quality reports, supplier scorecards)
- Propose pilot scope: one production line, one shift, 30-day measurement

### Week 3--4: Pilot Deployment
- Deploy Predictive Maintenance on target equipment
- Connect Quality Prediction to existing quality inspection data
- Integrate Supply Chain Risk with current supplier management system

### Week 5--6: ROI Measurement
- Measure downtime reduction, defect rate improvement, supply chain alert accuracy
- Produce ROI report with financial impact quantification
- Present results to VP Operations and plant leadership

### Week 7--8: Expansion Proposal
- Propose full-plant deployment based on pilot results
- Bundle additional tools (Safety Compliance Monitor, Asset Utilization Optimizer)
- Structure annual contract with governance layer attachment
