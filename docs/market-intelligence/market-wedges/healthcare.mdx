## Entry Product Bundle

The healthcare wedge enters with three tools that address the industry's most expensive operational and compliance burdens:

| Tool | Chokepoint Addressed | Typical Monthly Cost of Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Clinical Decision Support** | Medical errors, treatment variability (NAICS primary problem) | $25K--$500K |
| **Claims Processing Accelerator** | Claims processing errors and delays (Chokepoint #11) | $15K--$300K |
| **Healthcare Compliance Engine** | Patient record compliance failures (Chokepoint #18) | $25K--$500K |

Combined, these three tools address $65K--$1.3M in monthly cost-of-inaction. At a $70K--$90K entry price, the buyer achieves ROI within 60--90 days.

---

## Why Healthcare in Phase 4

Healthcare enters in Phase 4 as the final proof of cross-industry capability:

1. **Regulatory intensity** -- Healthcare is among the most heavily regulated industries (HIPAA, CMS, FDA, state boards). Proving the governance layer works here validates it for every other regulated sector.
2. **Administrative waste** -- Healthcare administrative costs consume 15--30% of total spending. AI that reduces administrative burden translates directly to patient care capacity.
3. **Claims economics** -- Claims processing represents the financial backbone of healthcare. Every 1% improvement in claims accuracy or speed translates to millions in recovered revenue for mid-size health systems.
4. **Sensitivity proof** -- Healthcare data is among the most sensitive. Proving the marketplace can operate with full governance, privacy, and compliance in healthcare demonstrates capability for any data sensitivity level.

## Sales Process

### Week 1--3: Discovery & Qualification
- Review claims denial rates and root causes by category
- Assess compliance audit history and outstanding remediation items
- Quantify administrative burden: hours per clinical staff member spent on documentation vs. patient care

### Week 4--6: Pilot Deployment
- Deploy Claims Processing Accelerator on highest-denial claims category
- Activate Healthcare Compliance Engine on one department's patient records
- Configure Clinical Decision Support for target clinical pathway

### Week 7--9: ROI Measurement
- Measure claims denial rate reduction and resubmission cycle time improvement
- Track compliance finding remediation progress
- Quantify administrative time savings for clinical staff
- Produce ROI report suitable for board and CMS review

### Week 10--12: Expansion Proposal
- Propose system-wide deployment across all departments and claims categories
- Bundle additional tools (Smart Scheduling Optimizer, Data Retention Governor)
- Structure annual contract with governance layer attachment (essential in healthcare -- the "fries" are non-negotiable)
