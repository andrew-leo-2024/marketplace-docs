## Modules

### Module 1: Research Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Literature Review Accelerator** | Systematic literature review | Research | Analytics | Lit reviews take months; AI synthesizes thousands of papers in hours with bias detection |
| **Grant Proposal Optimizer** | Grant writing & submission | Research Administration | Finance | Grant success rates 10-20%; AI optimizes proposals against funder priorities |
| **Research Collaboration Matcher** | Researcher networking | Collaboration | R&D | Researchers don't know who's working on adjacent problems; AI matches globally |
| **Experiment Design Assistant** | Research methodology | Research Design | R&D | Poor experimental design wastes funding; AI validates methodology and identifies confounds |
| **IP Commercialization Engine** | Technology transfer | Innovation/IP | Finance | 95% of university patents go unlicensed; AI matches IP to commercial opportunities |

### Module 2: Education Operations

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Adaptive Learning Orchestrator** | Curriculum delivery | Teaching | Education | One-size-fits-all teaching; AI personalizes learning paths per student |
| **Student Outcome Predictor** | Student success monitoring | Student Services | Analytics | At-risk students identified too late; AI predicts dropout risk from behavioral signals |
| **Accreditation Compliance Automator** | Accreditation reporting | Compliance | Regulatory | Accreditation reports consume months; AI continuously collects evidence and generates reports |
| **Research Impact Quantifier** | Impact measurement | Analytics | Performance | "Publish or perish" metrics broken; AI measures real-world impact beyond citations |
| **Lab Resource Optimizer** | Lab management | Operations | Facilities | Expensive lab equipment sits idle 60%+ of time; AI optimizes scheduling across researchers |


## Systemic Gaps

Publish-or-perish dysfunction, grant dependency, slow knowledge transfer, accreditation burden, adjunct exploitation, student debt, research reproducibility crisis, IP undercommercialization.
