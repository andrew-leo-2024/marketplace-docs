## Modules

### Module 1: Investment & Portfolio Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Alternative Investment Analyzer** | Due diligence on alternatives | Investment Analysis | Finance | PE/VC/RE due diligence is manual and slow; AI analyzes deal flow, comps, and risks |
| **Co-Investment Network Engine** | Co-investment sourcing | Deal Sourcing | Finance | Deal flow limited to existing networks; AI matches co-investment opportunities |
| **Tax-Efficient Structuring Advisor** | Tax planning | Tax Management | Legal/Finance | Multi-jurisdiction tax optimization requires armies of advisors; AI models structures |
| **Liquidity & Cash Flow Predictor** | Cash management | Treasury | Finance | Illiquid portfolios create cash crunches; AI predicts liquidity needs across holdings |
| **ESG & Impact Scoring Engine** | Impact measurement | ESG Compliance | Analytics | ESG reporting fragmented; AI scores portfolio across environmental, social, governance metrics |

### Module 2: Family Office Operations

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Consolidated Reporting Platform** | Multi-entity reporting | Financial Reporting | Finance | Reporting across 50+ entities is manual; AI consolidates and normalizes |
| **Vendor & Service Provider Optimizer** | Vendor management | Operations | Procurement | Family offices overpay for services; AI benchmarks fees and negotiates |
| **Family Governance Facilitator** | Family meeting & decision support | Governance | Administration | Family disputes destroy wealth; AI facilitates structured decision-making |
| **Next-Gen Education & Integration** | Heir preparation | Education | HR/Development | Next generation unprepared; AI-curated learning paths tied to family portfolio |
| **Cybersecurity & Privacy Shield** | Personal security management | Security | IT/Security | HNW families are prime targets; AI monitors threats across digital footprint |


## Systemic Gaps

Principal-agent problem, fragmented reporting, succession planning avoidance, cybersecurity underinvestment, illiquidity traps, family conflict, regulatory complexity.
