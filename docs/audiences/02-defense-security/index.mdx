## Modules

### Module 1: Intelligence & Analysis

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Multi-Source Intelligence Fusion** | OSINT/SIGINT/HUMINT synthesis | Intelligence Analysis | Analytics | Analysts drowning in data; AI fuses signals across sources and highlights anomalies |
| **Threat Pattern Recognition Engine** | Threat assessment | Early Warning | Security | Threats detected too late; AI identifies emerging patterns from weak signals |
| **Strategic Scenario Modeler** | Wargaming & scenario planning | Strategic Planning | Planning | Scenario planning limited by human cognitive bandwidth; AI generates 1000+ scenarios |
| **Secure Communications Analyzer** | Communications monitoring | SIGINT | Intelligence | Volume of intercepted communications exceeds human analysis capacity |
| **Adversary Behavior Predictor** | Predictive intelligence | Threat Intelligence | Analytics | Reactive posture; AI models adversary decision patterns and predicts next moves |

### Module 2: Operational Security

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cyber Threat Hunting Platform** | Threat detection & response | Cybersecurity | Security | APTs dwell undetected for months; AI hunts lateral movement and anomalous patterns |
| **Supply Chain Integrity Verifier** | Defense supply chain audit | Supply Chain Security | Procurement | Compromised components in defense supply chains; AI traces provenance and flags risks |
| **Personnel Security Continuous Eval** | Security clearance monitoring | Personnel Security | HR/Security | Point-in-time clearance checks miss ongoing risks; continuous behavioral monitoring |
| **Disinformation Detection Engine** | Information warfare defense | Information Ops | Security | State-sponsored disinformation campaigns; AI identifies coordinated inauthentic behavior |
| **Autonomous System Kill-Chain Auditor** | Autonomous weapons oversight | Weapons Governance | Compliance | Autonomous weapons need human-in-the-loop verification; AI enforces ETLB compliance |


## Systemic Gaps

Classification barriers, legacy system integration (50-year-old systems), acquisition cycle length (7-15 years), vendor lock-in, interoperability failure, insider threat detection.
