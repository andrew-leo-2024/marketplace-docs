## Modules

### Module 1: Industry Intelligence & Advocacy

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Industry Benchmarking Engine** | Competitive benchmarking | Research & Analysis | Analytics | Benchmarking relies on voluntary surveys with 20% response rates; AI synthesizes public data |
| **Regulatory Impact Modeler** | Policy advocacy | Government Relations | Policy | Advocacy is qualitative; AI quantifies impact of proposed regulations on industry members |
| **Skills Gap Analyzer** | Workforce development | Education/Training | HR | Industry doesn't know its own skills gaps; AI maps demand vs supply by role and region |
| **Trade Dispute Intelligence** | Trade policy analysis | International Trade | Policy | Tariff and trade dispute impacts poorly understood; AI models cascading effects |
| **Industry Standards Compiler** | Standards development | Standards Management | Governance | Standards take 5+ years to develop; AI accelerates drafting from best-practice synthesis |

### Module 2: Member Services Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Member Engagement Predictor** | Membership retention | Member Relations | Analytics | Members leave silently; AI identifies disengagement patterns and triggers intervention |
| **Event & Conference Optimizer** | Event management | Events | Operations | Conference ROI unmeasured; AI optimizes programming, matchmaking, and sponsorship |
| **Collective Bargaining Intelligence** | Negotiation support | Labor Relations | Legal | Negotiation positions poorly data-supported; AI models scenarios and precedents |
| **Supply Chain Sector Monitor** | Industry supply chain tracking | Supply Chain | Analytics | Industry bodies blind to systemic supply chain risks; AI monitors across members |
| **Innovation Radar** | Technology trend monitoring | R&D/Innovation | Strategy | Industries disrupted because they detect threats too late; AI scans signals globally |


## Systemic Gaps

Relevance erosion, data poverty, talent competition, consensus paralysis, slow cycle time, revenue dependency on membership dues, inability to enforce standards.
