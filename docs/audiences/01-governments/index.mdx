## Modules

### Module 1: Sovereign AI Governance Stack

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Policy Compiler Engine** | Legislative drafting & review | Policy Development | Governance | Manual policy creation takes months; AI compiles policy from intent statements in hours |
| **Regulatory Impact Analyzer** | Regulatory impact assessment | Policy Analysis | Compliance | Impact assessments are subjective and slow; AI models scenario consequences across sectors |
| **Citizen Service Orchestrator** | Service delivery automation | Public Services | Operations | Citizen-facing services fragmented across 50+ agencies; AI routes and resolves |
| **Inter-Ministry Coordination Platform** | Cross-agency coordination | Administrative Coordination | Coordination | Ministries operate in silos; creates shared decision surface with audit trails |
| **Budget Allocation Optimizer** | National budget planning | Financial Management | Finance | Budget allocation driven by politics not data; AI models outcome-per-dollar across programs |
| **Legislative Language Harmonizer** | Cross-jurisdictional alignment | Legal Harmonization | Legal | Laws conflict across jurisdictions; AI identifies contradictions and proposes harmonization |
| **Public Procurement Intelligence** | Procurement & tendering | Supply Chain | Procurement | Procurement fraud, bid-rigging, and vendor lock-in; AI scores bids on value not relationships |
| **National Data Sovereignty Vault** | Data governance | Information Security | Infrastructure | Government data scattered across foreign cloud providers; sovereign storage with AI indexing |
| **Grant & Subsidy Fraud Detector** | Disbursement verification | Fraud Prevention | Audit | Billions lost to fraudulent claims; AI cross-references applications against registry data |
| **Constitutional Compliance Checker** | Legal review | Constitutional Law | Legal | Proposed legislation may violate constitutional provisions; AI flags conflicts pre-drafting |

### Module 2: National AI Safety & Ethics

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **AI Deployment Authorization System** | AI system approval workflow | AI Governance | Regulatory | No standardized process for approving AI deployments in government; creates gated approval |
| **Algorithmic Bias Auditor** | Fairness assessment | Ethics & Compliance | Audit | Government AI systems may discriminate; automated bias detection across protected classes |
| **Citizen Privacy Impact Modeler** | Privacy impact assessment | Data Protection | Privacy | GDPR/PDPA compliance is manual and inconsistent; AI models privacy risks before deployment |
| **AI Incident Response Coordinator** | Incident management | Crisis Management | Operations | No playbook when government AI fails; automated escalation, containment, and communication |
| **Sovereign AI Registry** | National AI inventory | Asset Management | Registry | Government doesn't know what AI it runs; centralized registry with risk scoring |

### Module 3: E-Government Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Citizen Intent Router** | Service request classification | Customer Service | Operations | Citizens don't know which agency handles their problem; AI classifies and routes |
| **Public Document Simplifier** | Document transformation | Communications | Content | Government documents incomprehensible to citizens; AI rewrites at appropriate literacy level |
| **Multi-Language Government Translator** | Translation & localization | Communications | Language | Multilingual nations struggle with document translation; AI maintains legal precision across languages |
| **National Statistics Accelerator** | Statistical analysis & reporting | Research & Analysis | Analytics | Census and survey analysis takes years; AI processes and models in weeks |
| **Smart City Operations Platform** | Urban infrastructure management | Infrastructure Ops | IoT/Operations | City systems (traffic, utilities, waste) uncoordinated; AI optimizes across systems |


## Systemic Gaps

Decision latency (months for approvals), accountability diffusion (no single owner), vendor dependency, data silos, regulatory lag, institutional memory loss, procurement corruption.
