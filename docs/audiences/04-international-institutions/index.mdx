## Modules

### Module 1: Global Coordination Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Treaty Compliance Monitor** | Treaty implementation tracking | Compliance Monitoring | Governance | Treaties signed but not implemented; AI tracks compliance across signatories |
| **SDG Progress Tracker** | Development goals monitoring | Performance Management | Analytics | SDG tracking relies on self-reported data with 2-3 year lag; AI synthesizes real-time indicators |
| **Cross-Border Obligation Router** | International obligation management | Coordination | Operations | "Money stays domestic, obligations go global" — AI routes and tracks cross-border commitments |
| **Multilateral Negotiation Simulator** | Diplomatic negotiation support | Strategic Planning | Planning | Complex multi-party negotiations; AI models positions, trade-offs, and convergence paths |
| **Humanitarian Response Optimizer** | Aid allocation | Resource Management | Operations | Aid misallocated due to political pressure; AI optimizes allocation by impact per dollar |

### Module 2: Institutional Governance

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Member State Reporting Harmonizer** | Standardized reporting | Data Management | Compliance | 193 member states report in different formats; AI normalizes and validates |
| **Peacekeeping Operations Dashboard** | Mission management | Operations Management | Operations | Complex multi-country missions lack real-time visibility; AI consolidates operational data |
| **International Standards Compiler** | Standards development | Standards Management | Governance | Standards development takes 5-10 years; AI accelerates drafting and harmonization |
| **Climate Finance Tracker** | Climate funding flows | Financial Tracking | Finance | $100B/year climate finance pledge untracked; AI traces flows from pledge to project |
| **Sanctions Compliance Engine** | Sanctions enforcement | Compliance | Legal | Sanctions regimes complex and overlapping; AI maps entities, relationships, and exposure |


## Systemic Gaps

Consensus paralysis (194 sovereign vetoes), data sovereignty conflicts, implementation gap, funding uncertainty, institutional inertia, accountability vacuum, coordination overhead.
