## Modules

### Module 1: Legacy Modernization Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Legacy System Migration Planner** | IT modernization | IT Strategy | Infrastructure | Decades-old systems too risky to replace; AI maps dependencies and sequences migration |
| **Process Mining & Optimization Engine** | Business process analysis | Operations | Analytics | "We've always done it this way" — AI discovers actual process flows vs documented ones |
| **Mainframe-to-Cloud Bridge** | Application modernization | IT Operations | Infrastructure | COBOL/mainframe systems can't integrate with modern AI; AI creates translation layers |
| **Tribal Knowledge Extractor** | Knowledge capture from retiring workforce | Knowledge Management | HR/Knowledge | 40% of workforce retiring in 10 years; AI captures undocumented expertise before it walks out |
| **Quality Prediction Engine** | Quality management | Manufacturing/QA | Operations | Defects detected post-production; AI predicts quality issues from upstream process data |

### Module 2: Legacy Operations Optimization

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Predictive Maintenance Platform** | Equipment maintenance | Asset Management | Operations | Unplanned downtime costs $260K/hour in manufacturing; AI predicts failures before they occur |
| **Inventory Optimization Engine** | Inventory management | Supply Chain | Finance | Excess inventory ties up capital; stockouts lose sales; AI optimizes dynamically |
| **Energy Consumption Optimizer** | Energy management | Facilities | Operations | Energy costs 20-30% of manufacturing cost; AI identifies waste patterns |
| **Supplier Dependency Risk Scorer** | Vendor risk management | Procurement | Risk | Single-source dependency creates fragility; AI scores and models alternative scenarios |
| **Compliance Documentation Generator** | Regulatory documentation | Compliance | Legal | ISO/FDA/EPA documentation is manual and error-prone; AI generates from process data |


## Systemic Gaps

Technical debt (20-40 years), tribal knowledge concentration, resistance to change, skill gap, integration complexity, regulatory burden, deferred maintenance.
