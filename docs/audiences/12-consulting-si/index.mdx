## Modules

### Module 1: Consulting Delivery Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Engagement Scoping Optimizer** | Project scoping & estimation | Sales/Delivery | Pre-Sales | Scope creep and underestimation; AI models effort from requirements and historical data |
| **Knowledge Reuse Engine** | Deliverable reuse | Knowledge Management | Operations | Same analysis repeated across clients; AI indexes past work and surfaces reusable components |
| **Benchmarking-as-a-Service** | Industry benchmarking | Analysis | Client Service | Benchmarking data expensive and stale; AI synthesizes real-time comparative data |
| **Due Diligence Automation Suite** | M&A and strategy due diligence | Analysis | Client Service | DD teams manually review thousands of documents; AI extracts risks and red flags |
| **Implementation Risk Predictor** | Project risk management | Delivery | Risk | SI projects fail 70% of the time; AI identifies risk factors from project patterns |

### Module 2: SI Operations Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Resource-to-Engagement Matcher** | Staffing optimization | HR/Operations | Operations | Bench costs and mismatches; AI matches consultant skills to engagement requirements |
| **Client Relationship Intelligence** | Account management | Sales | CRM | Relationships depend on partner personal networks; AI maps and monitors client health |
| **Proposal Generation Engine** | RFP response automation | Business Development | Sales | Proposals take weeks; AI generates tailored responses from templates and past wins |
| **Margin & Utilization Optimizer** | Financial management | Finance | Operations | Utilization below 70% destroys margin; AI optimizes staffing and pricing |
| **Training & Certification Tracker** | Professional development | HR/Learning | HR | Consultants' skills stale; AI tracks certifications and recommends upskilling |


## Systemic Gaps

Billable-hour misalignment, knowledge walking out the door, scale-quality trade-off, commoditization of strategy, AI threatening core business model, bench costs, proposal fatigue.
