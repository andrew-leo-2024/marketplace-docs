## Modules

### Module 1: Deal Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Deal Flow Scoring Engine** | Investment sourcing & screening | Deal Sourcing | Analytics | 99% of deals are noise; AI scores opportunities against thesis, market, and team quality |
| **Portfolio Company Health Monitor** | Portfolio management | Portfolio Ops | Analytics | Board reports quarterly and curated; AI monitors real-time health from financial/operational data |
| **Market Timing Analyzer** | Market cycle analysis | Investment Strategy | Analytics | Entry/exit timing drives returns; AI models market cycles and signals |
| **LP Reporting Automator** | Investor relations reporting | Fund Operations | Finance | LP reports take weeks to prepare; AI generates from portfolio data continuously |
| **Exit Scenario Modeler** | Exit planning | Investment Strategy | Planning | Exit timing and method suboptimal; AI models IPO/M&A/secondary scenarios |

### Module 2: Investment Operations Intelligence

| Tool/System | Business Process | Business Function | Category | Problem Solved |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Competitive Landscape Mapper** | Market analysis | Research | Analytics | Competitive intelligence fragmented; AI maps competitors, positioning, and market dynamics |
| **Founder Assessment Engine** | Team evaluation | Due Diligence | HR/Analytics | Founder evaluation biased and subjective; AI assesses track record, network, and capability |
| **Term Sheet Analyzer** | Deal structuring | Legal/Finance | Legal | Complex term sheets hide adverse terms; AI flags non-standard provisions and precedents |
| **Fund Performance Attribution** | Performance analytics | Fund Management | Finance | Attribution between market beta and alpha unclear; AI decomposes returns |
| **Syndicate Coordination Platform** | Co-investment management | Operations | Coordination | Multi-investor deals require complex coordination; AI streamlines communication and documentation |


## Systemic Gaps

Power law returns, information asymmetry, herd mentality, vintage year dependency, LP pressure, fee structure misalignment, lack of operational support, founder-investor trust breakdown.
